✨ Blue Theme Characteristics

  • Bold Brand Presence: IBM Blue (#0f62fe) dominates the entire interface
  • High Contrast: White content cards pop dramatically against the blue background
  • Professional & Distinctive: Unmistakably IBM-branded appearance
  • Modern Card Design: Elevated white cards create clear content hierarchy
  • Cohesive Experience: Seamless blue throughout header, body, and footer
  • Strong Visual Impact: Eye-catching and memorable design
  • Semi-transparent Elements: Subtle inactive tabs blend with the blue background
  • Excellent Readability: White cards ensure content remains easy to read
