💡 Why Table-Based Layout?

  • Outlook Compatibility: Microsoft Outlook (especially Windows) uses Word's rendering engine, which handles tables better than modern CSS
  • Predictable Sizing: Fixed widths prevent unexpected shrinking or expanding
  • Cross-Platform Consistency: Works identically on Mac, Windows, and web versions of Outlook
  • Industry Standard: All major email marketing platforms (Mailchimp, Constant Contact, etc.) use table-based layouts
